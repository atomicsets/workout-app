Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Workout Tracker ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data.

Data Controller

Atomic Sets operates from the European Union and complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For authentication services, we use Supabase, with data stored in AWS EU-West (Ireland), ensuring your data remains within the European Union.

Information We Collect

We take a privacy-first approach and collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services.

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account (for authentication purposes only)
  • Subscription Information: Payment and subscription status (processed and stored by third-party payment providers; we do NOT store payment card details)

Information We DO NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Workout Data: All workout logs, exercise history, sets, reps, weights, and personal notes are stored ONLY on your device
  • Personal Health Information: We do not collect any health or fitness data
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, operating system details, or device-specific information
  • Usage Analytics: We do not track your app usage patterns or behavior
  • Location Data: We do not collect or access your location information

How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect only for:

  • Account Authentication: To verify your identity and allow you to log in
  • Subscription Management: To manage your premium subscription status and access to premium features
  • Essential Communications: To send critical account-related notifications (e.g., password reset, subscription expiry)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do NOT use your information for:

  • Marketing or promotional purposes
  • Analytics or behavior tracking
  • Profiling or personalized advertising
  • Sharing with third parties (except as legally required)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

Your Workout Data Stays on Your Device:

  • All workout data (exercises, sets, reps, weights, notes, templates, history) is stored ONLY on your device using encrypted local storage
  • We do NOT sync, backup, or transmit your workout data to any cloud servers
  • You maintain complete control over your workout data at all times
  • Your data never leaves your device

Backup Your Data

Since your workout data is stored locally:

  • iOS Users: Use iCloud Backup to automatically back up your app data (managed by Apple, subject to Apple's privacy policy)
  • Android Users: Use Google Backup or your device's backup solution (managed by Google, subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Manual Export: You can export your data from within the app for your own backup purposes

What We Store (Minimal Cloud Data)

We only store on our secure servers:

  • Email address and hashed password: For account authentication
  • Subscription status: To verify premium access (no payment details)

Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL): All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted
  • Secure password hashing: We use industry-standard bcrypt or similar algorithms
  • No data collection: Since we don't collect workout data, it can't be compromised on our servers
  • Regular security updates: We maintain and update our authentication systems

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Since we collect minimal data and your workout data stays on your device, there is very little to share. We may share the limited account data we collect only in these circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Authentication Services: For secure login functionality
  • Payment Processors: For subscription management (e.g., Apple App Store, Google Play Store) - they handle all payment data
  • Server Hosting: For storing account credentials

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your account information when:

  • Required by law, court order, or governmental regulation
  • Necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
  • Needed to investigate fraud, security issues, or violations of our Terms of Service

Business Transfers

  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your account data (email and subscription status) may be transferred
  • You will be notified beforehand and given the option to delete your account

What We Never Share

We never share:

  • Your workout data (it's only on your device)
  • Your personal health information
  • Your usage patterns or analytics data

Your Rights and Choices

Your Workout Data (On Your Device)

Since all your workout data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • View Your Data: Access all your workout data within the App at any time
  • Update Information: Modify your exercises, workouts, and preferences freely
  • Export Data: Export your workout data for backup or transfer purposes
  • Delete Data: Remove specific workout entries, exercises, or all data from your device
  • Backup: Use iOS iCloud Backup or Android backup solutions to protect your data

Account Management

  • View Account Info: Access your email and subscription status in the App
  • Delete Account: Permanently delete your account through the App settings
    • This removes your email and subscription data from our servers
    • Your local workout data remains on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • Data Retention: Deleted account data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days, except where legal retention is required (e.g., financial records for tax compliance)

Communications

  • Essential Only: We only send critical account-related emails (password reset, subscription notifications)
  • No Marketing: We do not send promotional or marketing communications
  • Opt-Out: You can manage notification preferences in App settings

Third-Party Services

The App integrates with minimal third-party services:

  • Authentication Services: For secure account management
  • Payment Processing: For premium subscriptions via Apple App Store or Google Play Store (all payment data is handled by Apple/Google; we never see or store your payment card details)
  • Device Backup Services: iOS iCloud Backup or Android backup solutions (optional; managed by Apple/Google)

We do NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Tracking or profiling services

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

  • We ensure adequate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • We comply with GDPR (for EU users) and other applicable data protection laws

Data Retention

On Our Servers (Minimal)

  • Active Accounts: Your email and subscription status are retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Account data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days
  • Legal Obligations: Financial records related to subscriptions may be retained longer for tax compliance (typically 5-7 years depending on jurisdiction)
  • Backups: Deleted account data is removed from backup systems within 90 days

On Your Device (Your Control)

  • Workout Data: Remains on your device indefinitely until you delete it
  • App Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all local workout data from your device
  • Device Backups: Your workout data may persist in iOS iCloud Backup or Android device backups according to your device settings
