Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my workout data stored?

All your workout data is stored locally on your device only. We do not sync or backup your data to cloud servers. Make sure to use iCloud Backup (iOS) or device backup (Android) to protect your data.

How do I backup my data?

Use the export feature in the app settings to create a backup file. You can also rely on iOS iCloud Backup or Android device backup to automatically back up your app data.

Can I sync my workouts across devices?

Currently, workout data is stored locally on your device. You can export from one device and import to another using the app's export/import feature.

What are premium features?

Premium features are currently available to all users. Check the app for the latest information on premium access.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account through the app settings. This will remove your email and account data from our servers. Your local workout data will remain on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the app.

Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Atomic Sets is designed with a privacy-first approach - your workout data never leaves your device.
